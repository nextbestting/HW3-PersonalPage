A closer look at the work I’ve done across finance, software, and entrepreneurship.

Experience

I’ve built experience at the intersection of analytics, operations, and product thinking. As a Finance Intern at Thomas H. Lee Partners, I supported reporting across multiple active funds and helped ensure deliverables were accurate and on time. I also wrote a Python script to track 320+ deals and categorize them in a way that made reimbursement calculations more reliable and easier to manage. That work taught me how small tools can unlock big efficiency when they’re aimed at real workflow pain points.

I’m also the CEO of TrustGod Apparel, where I merged my technical background with business execution to launch and run a mission-driven brand. I built and maintained the e-commerce platform (Shopify + web fundamentals), optimized for better user experience, and launched a charity program that allocates a portion of profits back to the community through social engagement. That’s where I learned the full loop: building, marketing, operating, and constantly iterating to improve.

Overall, my style is “hands-on builder.” I like clear systems, measurable outcomes, and work that’s fast enough to ship—while still being clean enough that I’d be proud to show it to a recruiter or teammate.
